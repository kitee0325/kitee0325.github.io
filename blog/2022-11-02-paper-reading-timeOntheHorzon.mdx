## Notes

This paper presents an excellent visual design: Horizon graph. This design solves the problem that the aspect ratio of single dimension is too high when multiple single dimension flow graphs are displayed side by side.
![](https://kitee-1301346990.cos.ap-nanjing.myqcloud.com/Obsidian/202411131557313.png?imageSlim)

Approach:

1. Use a conspicuous ladder color split the value
   ![](https://kitee-1301346990.cos.ap-nanjing.myqcloud.com/Obsidian/202411131605403.png?imageSlim)
2. Positive and negative values share vertical space(1/2 height saved)
   ![](https://kitee-1301346990.cos.ap-nanjing.myqcloud.com/Obsidian/202411131605182.png?imageSlim)
3. Collapse the color bands (1/3 height saved)
   ![](https://kitee-1301346990.cos.ap-nanjing.myqcloud.com/Obsidian/202411131606017.png?imageSlim)

**total use 1/6 height of original**
